Directions

Hyde Memorial Observatory is located on the south side of Holmes Lake in Southeast Lincoln, Nebraska. From downtown, take Normal Boulevard all the way east to South 70th Street , then turn south (right) and continue past the North Shore Road entrace to the South Shore enterance to Holmes Lake Park. This is the same entereance used to get to the golf course. Turn west (right) onto South Shore Road and continue past the golf course turnoff until you see the observatory on your left. Additonal information can be obtained by calling the Lincoln Parks and Recreation Department at 441-8708.

Please Note:

The South Shore of Holmes Lake normally closes at 6pm, however it will be kept open on those nights when Hyde Observatory is open, for Hyde Observatory patrons only. For the current programs and schedule call 402-441-7094 or see our About Hyde page.

Special Arrangements

Special arrangements can be made for groups of 20 or more (clubs, church groups, classes, or even just a group of friends) on nights other than the regularly scheduled Saturday nights. The observatory can be opened for special groups or an observatory staff member may be able to visit your group to give a presentation or sky tour.

To make special arrangements call 402-441-7895 (weekdays 8:30am–5pm) at least two weeks in advnace. There is no charge, although a small honorarium may be requested for visiting speakers.
